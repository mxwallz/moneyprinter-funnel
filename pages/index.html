03 · BRAND GUIDELINE (SHORT)

Early-2000s community bank that prints your money.

Warm, light, institutional-with-a-wink, Y2K dot-matrix. Premium, never arcade. The bank framing buys trust; the Y2K layer gives the wink. Curiosity comes from the design; greed and AI come from the copy.

PALETTE
NOTE CREAM#e9e3d3 · ground
HALFTONE TAUPE#b7a888 · mesh
MOD RUST#c0512a · labels
TELLER MAROON#5e2d2d · structure
CURRENCY GREEN#0c8f66 · money
SEAL GOLD#E5B567 · foil only
ELECTRIC TEAL#06B6D4 · the AI
INK#241f1a · text

TYPE

  • Headlines — engraved serif (Caslon / Tiempos)
  • Data, labels, counters — monospace
  • Body — clean sans (reads 2026, not cosplay)

    • MOTION

  • Input top → machine works → value prints out, number ticks up
  • Loaders are printing. Never a spinner.
  • Numbers count up from zero (odometer). Reveals print line-by-line.
  • Stepped & mechanical Y2K. No smooth modern eases.

    • DO

  • ✓ Premium, warm, mechanical, cohesive
  • ✓ Bank UX language: Window 01, Now Serving, member-owned, gold seal
  • ✓ Mascot: chunky Y2K teller-bot, torso is a printer, treads, no name
  • ✓ Hard offset shadows (4–7px, no blur), 2px ink borders, squared corners
  • ✓ It prints CASH

    • DON'T

  • × Arcade, meme, slot-machine, neon
  • × Matrix code rain, floating shapes (globe, arrows)
  • × Dark-navy SaaS command-center
  • × Smooth modern fades / soft eases
  • × Dry deadpan-bank copy (copy stays hot)

    • VOICE

  • Greed leads, curiosity pulls, FOMO frames — everything hangs off the user's number.
  • AI is the mechanism, named via Ryan as credibility and "why now." Revealed progressively, never the headline.
  • North star: Yield Farming "money printer" VSL energy (greedy, blunt). Not deadpan-bank.
  • Compliance: income only in third-person case studies; user states their own number; sell the deliverable, not a promised result; every claim carries a number.
